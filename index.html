Where It All Started
Halloween in Burbank Map
Every year Burbank, CA, transforms into the most decorated neighborhood in the US, and becomes a literal Halloween Town. Entertainment industry pros build full sets and haunted houses in their front yards, often with live actors. This is the map that started it all and the inspiration behind FrightSpot.

About This Event
Terror Vision Haunted Manor is one of Burbank's most legendary professional haunted houses. Built by entertainment industry pros, this full walk-through experience features live actors, Hollywood-level set design, and immersive horror sequences.

New scenes added every weekend. Group rates available. Street parking nearby.
